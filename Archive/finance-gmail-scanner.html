How to use with the Dashboard

Step 1: Set your date range and click Scan Gmail above.
Step 2: Review the found transactions — uncheck any you want to exclude.
Step 3: Click "Export Selected → Dashboard" to download a JSON file.
Step 4: Open your Finance Dashboard, click "Import JSON" in the top bar, and select the exported file.
Step 5: The dashboard will merge the new transactions, skipping any duplicates.

Tip: Run this scanner monthly to keep your dashboard up to date. You can re-scan the same period safely — duplicates are always detected and skipped.
